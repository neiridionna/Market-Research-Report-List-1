<p><ul><li>Branch/Feeder AFCI</li><li>Combination AFCI (CAFCI)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AFCI receptacle market is categorized into three main types: Branch/Feeder AFCI, Combination AFCI (CAFCI), and Others. Branch/Feeder AFCI devices protect the entire branch circuit from arc faults, ensuring safety across multiple outlets. Combination AFCI (CAFCI) units offer enhanced protection by detecting both parallel and series arc faults, making them versatile for various applications. The "Other" category includes specialized or emerging AFCI technologies not classified in the first two types, catering to specific market needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
