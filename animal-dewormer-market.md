<p><strong>The Animal Dewormer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal dewormer market is categorized into two primary types: external use and internal use. External use dewormers are applied topically to control external parasites like fleas, ticks, and mites, providing a protective barrier for pets. In contrast, internal use dewormers are administered orally or through injections to eliminate internal parasites such as worms. Both types are essential for ensuring the health and well-being of animals, preventing diseases, and enhancing their overall quality of life.</p></p>
