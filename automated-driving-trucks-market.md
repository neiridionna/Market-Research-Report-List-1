<p><ul><li>Level 1</li><li>Level 2</li><li>Level 3</li><li>Level 4 and 5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated driving truck market is categorized into five levels based on automation. Level 1 features basic driver assistance, like adaptive cruise control. Level 2 allows for partial automation, where the system can control steering and acceleration but requires driver supervision. Level 3 enables conditional automation, allowing trucks to operate independently in specific scenarios, though a driver must be ready to take control. Levels 4 and 5 represent high and full automation, respectively, with Level 4 operating in defined areas and Level 5 being fully autonomous in all conditions.</p></p>
