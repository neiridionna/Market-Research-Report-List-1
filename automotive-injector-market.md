<p><p>The automotive injector market comprises several types that enhance engine performance and efficiency. Single-point injection uses one injector for multiple cylinders, delivering fuel to a central point. Continuous injection maintains a constant fuel flow, improving efficiency. Sequential injection fires injectors individually for precise fuel delivery at different engine cycles. Direct injection delivers fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing power and reducing emissions. Other types may include hybrid systems or alternative fuel injectors, catering to diverse automotive needs and technologies.</p></p>
