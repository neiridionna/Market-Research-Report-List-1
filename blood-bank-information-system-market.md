<p><p>A Blood Bank Information System (BBIS) is a specialized software designed to manage various functions within blood banks efficiently. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as integration with electronic health records (EHR) and automation, are shaping the future of the market. There is also a growing emphasis on improving patient safety and traceability, which further propels the adoption of BBIS. Moreover, collaboration between blood banks and healthcare institutions is becoming essential for optimal blood management.
