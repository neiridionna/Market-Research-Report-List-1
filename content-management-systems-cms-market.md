The market is divided into two primary types: web-based and on-premises. Web-based CMS solutions are hosted in the cloud, offering easy access and scalability, making them ideal for businesses seeking flexibility. On-premises CMS, conversely, are installed locally on servers, providing enhanced control and security but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types serve distinct organizational needs.</p></p>
