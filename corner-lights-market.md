<p><ul><li>Halogen/Incandescent</li><li>Xenon/HID</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corner lights can be categorized into three main types based on their lighting technology: Halogen/Incandescent, Xenon/HID, and LED. Halogen and incandescent lights provide warm illumination but consume more energy and have shorter lifespans. Xenon or HID lights produce bright, intense light with improved efficiency and longevity, ideal for enhanced visibility. LED corner lights offer the best energy efficiency, longevity, and versatility in design, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles due to their low heat emission and superior brightness.</p></p>
