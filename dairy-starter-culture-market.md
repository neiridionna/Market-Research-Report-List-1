<p><p>The dairy starter culture market is segmented into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotics. Mesophilic cultures thrive at moderate temperatures, usually for producing soft cheeses and yogurt. Thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures, ideal for making hard cheeses and other products requiring heat stability. Probiotic cultures are specifically designed to enhance gut health by adding beneficial bacteria to dairy products, promoting health benefits beyond basic fermentation. Together, these cultures cater to diverse dairy production needs and health preferences.</p></p>
