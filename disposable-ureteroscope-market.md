<p><p>The disposable ureteroscope market comprises two main types: flexible ureteroscopes and rigid ureteroscopes. Flexible ureteroscopes are characterized by their adaptable construction, allowing for easy navigation through the urinary tract for procedures like stone removal. In contrast, rigid ureteroscopes are inflexible and typically used in specific cases where durability and direct visualization are essential. Both types are designed for single-use, enhancing infection control and reducing sterilization costs, thus improving efficiency in urological procedures.</p></p>
