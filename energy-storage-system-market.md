<p><p>The energy storage system market comprises three primary types: electrochemical, thermal, and mechanical. Electrochemical storage, like batteries, stores energy as chemical potential, while thermal storage captures heat for later use, often in utilities and industrial settings. Mechanical energy storage, including technologies like pumped hydro and flywheels, relies on physical movement to store energy. Each type caters to different applications, from grid stability to renewable energy integration, enhancing efficiency and reliability in energy supply and demand management.</p></p>
