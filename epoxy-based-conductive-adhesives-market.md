<p><p>The epoxy-based conductive adhesives market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component conductive adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that require no additional components for application, offering convenience and ease of use. In contrast, two-component conductive adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed prior to application, allowing for customizable curing times and enhanced performance. Both types are utilized in electronic assembly, automotive, and aerospace industries, where strong electrical connectivity and bonding are critical.</p></p>
