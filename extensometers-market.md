<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Extensometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Extensometers</li><li>Non-contact Extensometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extensometer market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact extensometers. Contact extensometers physically attach to the test specimen, measuring deformation through direct contact, making them suitable for various applications, including mechanical testing and material characterization. In contrast, non-contact extensometers utilize optical technologies, such as laser or digital imaging, to capture displacement remotely, offering advantages in precision and avoiding potential interference with the specimen. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including construction, robotics, and materials science.</p></p>
