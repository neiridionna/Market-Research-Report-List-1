<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VA (Veno-Arterial) ECMO Services</li><li>VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECMO service market is categorized into two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is utilized for patients with severe cardiac and respiratory failure, providing support to both the heart and lungs by diverting blood away from the body, oxygenating it, and returning it to the arterial system. VV ECMO, on the other hand, is primarily focused on supporting patients with severe respiratory failure, enabling oxygenation while maintaining venous blood flow. Both types are critical in managing life-threatening conditions.</p></p>
