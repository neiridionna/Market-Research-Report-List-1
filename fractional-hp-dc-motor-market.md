<p><p>The fractional horsepower (HP) DC motor market encompasses two primary types: fractional brushed and fractional brushless motors. Fractional brushed motors use brushes to transfer electrical energy to the rotor, offering simple control and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for low-power applications. In contrast, fractional brushless motors employ electronic controllers to operate, providing greater efficiency, higher reliability, and reduced maintenance. This segment is ideal for applications requiring precision and durability, such as robotics and automotive systems, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions.</p></p>
