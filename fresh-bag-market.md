<p><p>Fresh bag market types include PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), and PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), each serving specific packaging needs. PE is widely used for its flexibility and moisture resistance, making it ideal for fresh produce. PVC offers strength and clarity but is more rigid, suitable for dry goods. PVDC provides superior barrier properties against gases and moisture, extending shelf life for perishable items. These materials cater to various packaging requirements, enhancing food preservation and consumer appeal.</p></p>
