<p><p>Gene amplification technologies encompass various methods used to replicate specific DNA sequences for analysis. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a widely utilized technique that employs thermal cycling to amplify DNA. Loop Mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) is another method that operates at a constant temperature, offering rapid results with minimal equipment. Other market types include various innovative techniques like CRISPR-based amplification and NAAT (Nucleic Acid Amplification Tests), catering to diverse applications in diagnostics, research, and biotechnology advancements.</p></p>
