<p><p>HIT (Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin layer) solar cells are classified into monofacial and bifacial types. Monofacial HIT cells capture sunlight solely from one side, making them suitable for installations with limited space. In contrast, bifacial HIT cells harness sunlight from both sides, allowing for increased energy generation, especially when positioned above reflective surfaces. The bifacial technology generally leads to higher efficiency, making it an attractive choice for large-scale solar projects. Both types enhance the overall performance of solar energy systems.</p></p>
