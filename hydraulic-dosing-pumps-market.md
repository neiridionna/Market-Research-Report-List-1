<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Pumps</li><li>Piston Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic dosing pumps market primarily includes diaphragm pumps and piston pumps, each serving specific applications. Diaphragm pumps use flexible membranes to control fluid flow, offering precise dosing and resistance to corrosive chemicals, making them ideal for water treatment and pharmaceutical industries. Piston pumps, on the other hand, utilize a reciprocating piston for high-pressure applications, ensuring accurate flow rates for more viscous fluids. Both types are essential for effective fluid management in various industrial processes, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
