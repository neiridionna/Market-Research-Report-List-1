<p><ul><li>Batch</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial frying system market comprises two main types: batch and continuous frying technologies. Batch frying involves processing food in discrete quantities, allowing for flexibility in production and easier flavor customization. It’s ideal for smaller operations or varied product lines. Continuous frying, on the other hand, operates non-stop, providing high efficiency and consistent quality for large-scale production. This method is favored for its ability to handle large volumes while maintaining uniform cooking results, making it suitable for mass production environments.</p></p>
