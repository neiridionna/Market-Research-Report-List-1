<p><p>The industrial paints market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Water-based paints** utilize water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly.</p><p>2. **Solvent-based paints** contain organic solvents, providing durable finishes suitable for harsh environments, though they emit higher VOCs.</p><p>3. **Powder coatings** are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, resulting in a tough, environmentally safe finish.</p><p>4. **Pre-treatment paint** involves surface preparation to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance, ensuring better performance of subsequent coatings.</p></p>
