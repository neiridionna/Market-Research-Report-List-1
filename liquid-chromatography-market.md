<p><p>Liquid chromatography encompasses several types tailored to specific analytical needs. High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) is widely used for its efficiency in separating compounds at moderate pressures. Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) takes this further, utilizing higher pressures for faster and more efficient separations. Low Pressure Liquid Chromatography (LPLC) is utilized for larger sample volumes and simpler compounds. Other market segments may include specialty techniques like preparative chromatography and biochromatography, addressing niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
