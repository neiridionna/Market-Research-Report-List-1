<p><ul><li>Heat Sterilization</li><li>Chemical Sterilization</li><li>Filter Sterilization</li><li>Ionizing Radiation Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical disinfection cabinet market encompasses several sterilization methods: Heat sterilization employs high temperatures to destroy pathogens, making it effective for metal instruments. Chemical sterilization uses gases or liquid chemicals to eliminate microorganisms, ideal for heat-sensitive items. Filter sterilization involves membrane filters that remove bacteria and spores from fluids, ensuring sterility in liquid preparations. Ionizing radiation sterilization uses gamma rays or electron beams to alter microbial DNA, providing a reliable method for single-use and sensitive equipment. Each method caters to specific needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
