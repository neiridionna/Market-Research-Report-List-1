<p><p>The metal disc filter market is primarily categorized into two types: wire mesh and laser drilled holes. Wire mesh filters consist of woven metal fibers that provide effective filtration while allowing for high flow rates. They are durable and reusable, making them ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, laser drilled hole filters have precise and customizable hole patterns, offering enhanced filtration efficiency and lower pressure drop. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and water treatment.</p></p>
