<p><p>The Metal Kammprofile Gasket market is segmented into two main types: Fixed Guide Ring Kammprofile and Floating Guide Ring Kammprofile. Fixed Guide Ring Kammprofile gaskets maintain a stationary guide ring that ensures consistent sealing under pressure, making them suitable for high-temperature and high-pressure applications. In contrast, Floating Guide Ring Kammprofile gaskets feature a movable guide ring, allowing for greater flexibility and adaptability to flange irregularities. Both types are essential for enhancing sealing performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
