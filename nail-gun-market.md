<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nail Gun</li><li>Combustion Powered Nail Gun</li><li>Electric Nail Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nail gun market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic, combustion-powered, and electric nail guns. Pneumatic nail guns utilize compressed air for high-speed nailing, favored for their power and efficiency in heavy-duty applications. Combustion-powered nail guns operate on fuel cartridges, providing mobility and independence from cords and compressors, ideal for remote job sites. Electric nail guns, which can be corded or cordless, offer convenience and ease of use for lighter tasks, making them popular among DIY enthusiasts and professionals alike.</p></p>
