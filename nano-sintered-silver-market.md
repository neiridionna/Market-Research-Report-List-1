<p><ul><li>Pressure Sinter</li><li>Non-Pressure Sinter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nano sintered silver market is categorized into two main types: pressure sinter and non-pressure sinter. Pressure sinter involves applying external pressure during the sintering process to enhance densification and improve conductivity, often resulting in superior mechanical properties. In contrast, non-pressure sinter utilizes heat alone without external pressure, making it simpler and cost-effective, but potentially yielding lower density. Each method offers distinct benefits, catering to various applications in electronics, photovoltaics, and other industries requiring high-performance silver materials.</p></p>
