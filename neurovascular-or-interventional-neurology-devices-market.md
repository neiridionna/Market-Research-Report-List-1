<p><p>The neurovascular or interventional neurology devices market encompasses various types aimed at treating cerebrovascular conditions. Cerebral thrombectomy devices facilitate the removal of blood clots from the brain's vessels. Carotid artery stenting systems enhance blood flow by placing stents in narrowed arteries. Cerebral aneurysm and AVM embolization systems target abnormal blood vessels for occlusion. Intracranial stent systems support artery walls to prevent collapse, while flow diverters redirect blood flow away from aneurysms, promoting healing and reducing risk.</p></p>
