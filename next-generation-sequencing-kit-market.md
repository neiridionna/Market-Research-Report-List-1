<p><p>The Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) kit market is primarily categorized into short-read sequencing and long-read sequencing. Short-read sequencing involves sequencing fragments of DNA that are typically 100-300 base pairs long, offering high throughput and accuracy, making it ideal for applications like whole-genome sequencing and targeted resequencing. In contrast, long-read sequencing enables the analysis of larger DNA fragments, often exceeding 10,000 base pairs, which facilitates complex genome assembly and structural variant detection, providing deeper insights into genomics and transcriptomics.</p></p>
