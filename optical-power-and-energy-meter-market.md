<p><p>The Optical Power and Energy Meter market comprises several types, notably pyroelectric meters, photodiode meters, and others. Pyroelectric meters measure optical power by detecting the temperature change caused by incident light, making them suitable for pulsed applications. Photodiode meters utilize semiconductor devices to generate an electrical current proportional to the light intensity, ideal for continuous wave measurements. Other types may include thermal or fiber optic sensors, each offering unique advantages for various applications in research, telecommunications, and industrial settings.</p></p>
