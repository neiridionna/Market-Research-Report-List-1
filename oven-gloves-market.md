<p><p>The oven gloves market comprises several types, each offering unique heat protection and comfort. Cotton gloves are breathable and easy to wash but may have lower heat resistance. Neoprene gloves provide excellent insulation and water resistance, making them ideal for wet environments. Silicone gloves offer flexibility and non-slip grip, while being heat resistant. Aramid fabric gloves are engineered for high-temperature resistance and durability, making them suitable for industrial use. Other materials include leather and blended fabrics, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
