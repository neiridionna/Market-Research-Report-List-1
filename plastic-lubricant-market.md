<p><strong>The Plastic Lubricant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Lubricant</li><li>Internal Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic lubricant market is divided into two main types: external and internal lubricants. External lubricants are applied to the surface of plastic products, enhancing their processing and reducing friction during manufacturing. They are commonly used for extrusion and molding operations. Internal lubricants, on the other hand, are integrated into the plastic formulation itself, improving flow and reducing viscosity during processing. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance, efficiency, and durability of plastic materials in various applications.</p></p>
