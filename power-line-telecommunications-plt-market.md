<p><ul><li>Narrowband</li><li>Broadband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Line Telecommunications (PLT) utilizes existing electrical wiring to transmit data alongside electrical power, enabling internet and communication services without extensive infrastructure investment. The market is categorized into narrowband and broadband segments. Narrowband PLT focuses on low data rate applications like smart metering and home automation, typically operating at frequencies below 500 kHz. In contrast, broadband PLT supports high-speed internet connectivity and multimedia applications by operating at higher frequencies, usually between 1.8 MHz and 100 MHz, catering to consumer and commercial demands.</p></p>
