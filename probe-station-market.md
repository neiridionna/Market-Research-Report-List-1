<p><p>The probe station market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automated, and automated systems. Manual probe stations require operators to position wafers and probes manually, offering flexibility but demanding more labor and time. Semi-automated probe stations streamline some processes, combining manual input with automated functions for improved efficiency. Automated probe stations eliminate manual tasks completely, providing high-throughput, precision testing for mass production. Each type caters to different needs in terms of cost, speed, and operational complexity within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
