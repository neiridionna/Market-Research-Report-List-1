<p><ul><li>Interior Train Lighting</li><li>Exterior Train Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway lighting market is divided into two main types: interior and exterior train lighting. Interior train lighting focuses on providing illumination within passenger compartments, ensuring safety, comfort, and visibility for travelers. It includes overhead lights and reading lamps, enhancing the passenger experience. Exterior train lighting, on the other hand, emphasizes the safety and visibility of the train itself during operation, featuring headlights, marker lights, and spotlights to improve operational efficiency and prevent accidents in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
