The equity REIT market focuses on owning and managing properties, generating revenue through leasing space and collecting rents. Mortgage REITs invest in real estate mortgages, earning income from interest on these loans. Hybrid REITs combine both equity and mortgage strategies, diversifying their portfolios to benefit from rental income and interest income, providing a balanced approach to real estate investing.</p></p>
