<p><p>The research stereo microscope market is categorized into three main types: monocular, binocular, and trinocular stereoscopic microscopes. Monocular stereoscopes feature a single eyepiece, making them compact and ideal for simple observation tasks. Binocular stereoscopes have two eyepieces, providing a three-dimensional view that enhances depth perception, suitable for detailed analysis. Trinocular stereoscopes include an additional port for cameras, allowing for imaging and documentation alongside live observation, catering to advanced research needs. Each type serves specific applications across various scientific fields.</p></p>
