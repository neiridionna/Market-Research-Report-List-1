<p><strong>The Smart Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Vehicle Barrier Gate market can be categorized into two main types: Straight and Crank gates. Straight barrier gates feature a horizontal arm that moves vertically to allow or restrict vehicle access, ideal for high-traffic areas. In contrast, Crank barrier gates operate with a manual or automatic crank mechanism, providing more flexibility in installation and operation. Both types enhance security and control in parking lots, toll stations, and restricted areas, catering to various needs in vehicle management.</p></p>
