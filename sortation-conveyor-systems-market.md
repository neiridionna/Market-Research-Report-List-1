<p><p>Sortation and conveyor systems market encompasses various types of systems designed for efficiently directing products to designated locations. Linear sortation systems operate in a straight line, utilizing gravity or powered conveyors to direct items to specific chutes or destinations, ideal for high-speed sorting in warehouses. In contrast, loop sortation systems incorporate a continuous loop design, allowing for versatile routing and high-density storage, making them suitable for complex distribution environments where space optimization and flexibility are critical.</p></p>
