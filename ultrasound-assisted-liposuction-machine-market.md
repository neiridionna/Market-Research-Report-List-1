<p><p>The Ultrasound Assisted Liposuction (UAL) market is categorized into two primary types: Internal UAL and External UAL. Internal UAL involves the use of ultrasound energy delivered through a cannula placed inside the body to liquefy fat cells for easier removal. In contrast, External UAL employs ultrasound waves emitted externally to target and emulsify fat deposits without invasive cannulas. Both methods enhance fat removal efficiency and promote smoother results, catering to varying patient needs and procedural preferences.</p></p>
