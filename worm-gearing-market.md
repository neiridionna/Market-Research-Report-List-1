<p><p>Worm gearing markets consist of three main types: </p><p>1. **Single Envelope Worm Gear**: Features a worm and a single-threaded worm wheel, providing compact design and high torque transmission, ideal for lower-speed applications. </p><p>2. **Double Envelope Worm Gear**: Combines two envelopes, enhancing load capacity and efficiency, suitable for demanding applications with higher torque and improved durability.</p><p>3. **Non-enveloping Worm Gear**: Utilizes non-conforming profiles, offering greater speed ratios, but with lower efficiency, typically used in light-load scenarios.</p></p>
